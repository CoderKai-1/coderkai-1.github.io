SHAPE SHIFTER

Steam Store Page

Game Development Godot/GDScript Solo Project Steam Multiplayer

Shape Shifter started as a 2D Level Editor I made in Godot for a school assignment
in January of 2024 but turned into a passion project of mine to explore the power
of Steam and the Steamworks-API.

Some of the base features include:
  • Level editor
  • Some tutorial demo levels for players to get started
  • Full controller & Steam Deck support (100% Steam Deck Verified)
  • Online multiplayer up to 16 people
  • Steam Workshop for people to upload their custom levels
  • Leaderboards of fastest times for individual online levels
  • Steam Achievements

Time to develop: About 3 months (January, November and December 2024)

Elite Space Repair Crew

Game Development Unreal Engine/C++ Solo Project

ESRC started as a project to learn the incredibly powerful Unreal Engine.
The game is a 3D first-person game where the player starts in a randomly-generated
corridor system and must repair as many broken components as they can before their
welding tool runs out of battery all while avoiding an evil robot that is chasing them.

Some of the base features include:
  • Randomly generated broken machinery that can be fixed with a welding tool
  • Adjustable settings such as volume, resolution and graphics
  • Randomly generated world map using Unreal Blueprints
  • Custom sound effects and music made by me in FL Studio

Time to develop: About 1.5 months (June - July 2024)
Note*: Almost all the models were not made by me
✖

Ace of Shades

Game Development Unity/C# VR

Ace Of Shades is a Virtual Reality (VR) game I made in Unity for a school assignment.
For this project I worked in a team of two, where my teammate made all the 3D
models, while I focused on the programming, audio design and world design.

Some of the base features include:
  • Voice acting and queued events
  • Valve Index grip controls
  • Music made in FL Studio
  • Multiple weapons with unique firing patterns
  • A boss fight with multiple waves and attacks

Time to develop: About 1 month (June 2023)
✖

Fragment

Game Development Godot/GDScript Solo Project

Fragment is a nonlinear 2D Side-scrolling Metroidvania I'm making in Godot.
The game will gradually introduce new mechanics that the player must learn to
use so they can explore more of the world and eventually beat a boss enemy.

  • Collectibles to guide the player
  • Basic tutorials for non-gamers
  • Dialogue system
  • 4 unique Abilities
  • Boss-fight that tests the player's skill
  • In-depth key rebinding system
  • grace mechanics (coyote time/jump buffering) and save points

Time to develop: About 3 months (August, November, December 2024 - Now)
